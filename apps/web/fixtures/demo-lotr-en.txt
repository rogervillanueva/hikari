The fellowship climbed the snowy pass in search of a path over the Misty Mountains.
Gimli tested each stone beneath his boots while Legolas listened to the whispers of the wind.
Frodo felt the weight of the Ring yet pressed on, determined not to fall behind his friends.
Sam clutched the cold handle of his cooking pot and prayed for the warmth of a kitchen once more.
Boromir steeled himself to shield the company even as he longed for his distant family.
Aragorn read the stars above and traced tomorrow's road within his mind.
Gandalf murmured ancient words, seeking to guard them from the dangers hidden in snow and night.
Their line stretched on with only snowlight to illuminate their faces.
The mountain met their challenge with deepening storms that blocked the way.
Still they trusted one another and carried the small flame of hope forward.
